Theater Costuming for the Beginner

Costuming is not like sewing a personal project you don't have time to invest into one project. You will need to have some time to get things planned and executed before a performance. Which is usually about 6 weeks.

Ideas for costumes

Ideas for a costume piece are knowing what you want something to look like and using other clothing items to make them look like your idea, a simple dress can be changed to fit any time period. Mens slacks can be changed as well.

In conclusion this is such a fun field to work. It is very satisfying to watch a character come together with the visual aid that costumes provide. 3/01/2021
